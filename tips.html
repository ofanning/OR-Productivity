Seven Tips To Help You Stay Focused!

1. Keep organized. Before you start working, make a To Do List! and ensure your workspace is decluttered!

2. Keep organized x2. Before you start working, ensure your workspace is decluttered!

3. No distractions. Turn off your social media notifications and put your phone somewhere out of sight!

4. Stay healthy. Make sure you eat food prior to working and stay hydrated!

5. Background noise. Figure out the sounds that help you focus. Some popular choices include white noise or jazz music!

6. Add color. Studies have shown that blue can increase focus and red increases attention to detail. Try to incorporate those colors in your workspace!

7. Know yourself. If you feel yourself losing focus, take a quick 15 minute break and return to your work.
